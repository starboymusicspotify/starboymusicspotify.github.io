About Me

Hi, I’m Starboy, a Nigerian hip-hop artist blending Afrobeat rhythms with modern hip-hop vibes. I was born in Lagos in 1998 and started making music in my teens. I’ve always been inspired by artists like Burna Boy and Wizkid, and I love creating energetic beats and catchy lyrics that connect with fans all over the world.

My Popular Track: Caro

"Caro" is my breakout hit. I wanted to capture my sound in a way that mixes infectious beats with my Nigerian hip-hop style. I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed making it.
