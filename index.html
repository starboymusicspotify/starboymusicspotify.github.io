About Starboy

Starboy is an innovative indie artist known for blending soulful melodies with modern electronic beats. Born in 1998, Starboy started making music in their bedroom studio at the age of 16. Inspired by artists like Frank Ocean and The Weeknd, Starboy’s music is emotional, atmospheric, and resonates deeply with fans worldwide.

Popular Track: Caro

"Caro" has captured fans with its heartfelt lyrics and smooth production, becoming Starboy's breakout hit.
